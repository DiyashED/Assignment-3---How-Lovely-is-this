Ben & Jerry's Milk and Cookies Ice Cream

Ben & Jerry's Milk and Cookies Ice Cream combines the creamy goodness of vanilla ice cream with chocolate chip and chocolate sandwich cookies. Perfect for cookie lovers, this ice cream offers a delightful crunch with every spoonful.

Interesting Facts!

  • Ben & Jerry's Milk and Cookies Ice Cream was inspired by the classic pairing of milk and cookies.
  • The ice cream features both chocolate chip and chocolate sandwich cookies for an extra crunch.
  • It's a fan favorite and often featured in limited edition collections.
